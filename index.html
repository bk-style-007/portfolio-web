Testimonials & Stories

Share Content

Covid-19

My role has involved leading the campus’s Covid-19 communications.

I created a dedicated Covid-19 webpage and keep it up to date.

I shared any important updates to our social media channels.

I also circulated all relevant updates and information to students and staff via email.

Photography

I completed a 20-week digital photography training certificate through the Nelson Marlborough Institute of Technology. I take portrait shots of students and graduates and photograph news and events for our channels.

Journalism

Prior to entering the communications field, I worked as a journalist for five years. I started my career at The Westport News on the South Island’s West Coast, then moved to NZME in Auckland.
